Organizing Committees

The most of the conferences around the world happens on the topics such as Science, Ecological Agriculture, Enviornmental sciences, Humanities, social sciences, Mathematics, etc.

There are 6 specialized committees which organizes the conferences which is welcomed by whole over the world. This committees have special permissions and also helps eligible people to contribute to the conferences by research papers, presentations, surveys, censuses, etc.

Below are the list of the committees which are responsible for organizing conferences on certain domain worldwide.

  1. The Institute of Research Engineers and Scientists (The IRES)

     Objective:- To promote and enhance the dialogue in education among the institutions devoted to Science and Engineering fields.

    Major types of conferences

    • Food Microbiology and Food Safety
    • Engineering and Natural science
    • Food and Agricultural Engineering
    • Tourism, Transport and Logistics
    • E-Education, E-Buisness, E-Management

  2. International Institute of Engineers and Researchers (IIER)

     Objective:- To Globalize the education for Research scholars from different regions and communicate witheach other through a common platform.

    Major types of conferences

    • Buisness Management
    • Applied physics and Mathematics
    • Law and Political Sciences
    • Psychology, Language and Teaching
    • Recent innovations in Engineering

  3. International Society for Engineering Research and Development(ISERD)

     Objective:- To encourage regional and international communication and collaboration; promote professional interaction and lifelong learning; recognize outstanding contributions of individuals and organizations; encourage scholar researchers to pursue studies and careers in circuit branches and its applications.

    Major types of conferences.

    • Chemical and Biochemical Engineering
    • Accounting and Finance
    • Education and E-learning
    • Economic, Management and Social studies
    • Natural Science

  4. The International Society for Engineers and Researchers (ISER)

     Objective:- It's mission is to promote and enhance the dialogue in education among the institutions devoted to Science, Engineering and Technology fields through Creation and dissemination of knowledge, Promotion of best practice standards in the service of international education.

    Major types of conferences.

    • Economics and Buisness Research
    • Chemical and enviornment Science
    • Agricultural and Biological Science
    • Advances in buisness management and Information Science
    • Nanoscience and Nanotechnology

  5. Global Society for Research and Development(GSRD)

     Objective:- To provide a common platform for all research scholars to present and showcase their research findings to the world during various conferences and seminars. To provide a unique academic networking opportunity

    Major types of conferences.

    • Agricultural, Biological and Environmental Science
    • Buisness, Humanities and Social Sciences
    • Medical, Pharmaceutical and Health services
    • Oil, Gas and Petrochemistry
    • Robotics, Aeronautics and Mechanics

  6. World Research Forum for advances in Science and Engineering (WRFASE)

     Objective:- The mission of the WRFASE is to promote Scientific and Engineering research that will improve the economic and social well-being of people around the world.

    Major types of conferences.

    • Ecological Agriculture and Forestry
    • Automation and Mechanical Engineering
    • Computer Science
    • Machine learning and Big Data
    • Artificial Intelligence and Robotics
